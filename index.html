Solar Panel Types

Monocrystalline

Highest efficiency (15-20%) but at a higher cost. Best for limited roof space.

Polycrystalline

Mid-range efficiency (13-16%) and cost. Good balance of price and performance.

Thin-Film

Lowest efficiency (10-13%) but lightweight and flexible. Best for large commercial installations.
